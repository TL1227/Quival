# Quival
A digital TCG where nobody goes first!

### Keywords
**SpellStream** - The spellstream is your action queue. Each phase you may select a number of
spells to play. They will be executed in that order at the same time as your opponents.

### CardKeywords
**SlowSpell** - Is automatically cast at the end of the SpellStream.

### Card Ideas
**Graveyard Shift:** _SlowSpell._ Select a creature from the graveyard and summon it to the
battlefield.  

**Bubble Shield:** Create a magical bubble that will absorb 1 source of damage from an opposing 
player before dissapearing.
