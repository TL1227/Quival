# Quival
A digital TCG where nobody goes first!

### Keywords
**Spellstream** - The spellstream is your action queue. Each phase you may select a number of
spells to play. They will be executed in that order at the same time as your opponents.
The spellstream has a total of 5 spellslots, plus a quickslot and slowslot. The layout is as follows.

[QuickSlot] -> [SpellSlot 1] -> [SpellSlot 2] -> [SpellSlot 3] -> [SpellSlot 4] -> [SpellSlot 5] -> [SlowSlot]

The quickslot always plays first, regardless of how many other spells are played.
The slowslot always plays last, regardless of how many other spells are played.

**SpellSlot** - A slot in the spellstream. _SpellSlot 2_ would refer to the second slot of the 
spell stream. Different creatures and spells may have different effects depending on the SpellSlot
they're cast.

### CardKeywords
**QuickSpell** - Can only be cast in the QuickSlot of the SpellStream.

**SlowSpell** - Can only be cast in the SlowSlot of the Spellstream.

**DownStream** - Add the current spellstream number to creatures next attack. 

**Aggressive** - Can choose to attack using the quickslot.

**Lethargic** - Can choose to attack using the slowslot.

### Card Ideas
**Graveyard Shift:** _SlowSpell._ Select a creature from the graveyard and summon it to the
battlefield.  

**Bubble Shield:** Create a bubble that will absorb 1 source of damage from an opposing 
player before dissapearing.

**Sprint Guard:** _QuickSpell._ Half the amount of the next incomming damage source.
